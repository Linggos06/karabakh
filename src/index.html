Victory Watch

Our army gave our people a glorious victory, ending 30 years of longing in 44 day. Every second on the pathway to Karabakh was like gold. During this time, our brave sons advanced round the clock. And ﬁnally, it’s time for the victory of our people!

27.09.2020

“Victory Watch” dedicated to our glorious success was tailor-made for the day of the beginning of this date in a limited number of 279 units.

X.XI.2020

Instead of 12-digit symbols indicating the time piece, the word AZERBAIJAN is depicted. The watch is completed with the numbers X and XI. These numbers are the result of our 44-day struggle, the moment of victory of our people!
