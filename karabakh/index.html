Victory Watch

Our army gave our people a glorious victory, ending 30 years of longing in 44 day. Every second on the pathway to Karabakh was like gold. During this time, our brave sons advanced round the clock. And ﬁnally, it’s time for the victory of our people!

27.09.2020

“Victory Watch” dedicated to our glorious success was tailor-made for the day of the beginning of this date in a limited number of 279 units.

X.XI.2020

Instead of 12-digit symbols indicating the time piece, the word AZERBAIJAN is depicted. The watch is completed with the numbers X and XI. These numbers are the result of our 44-day struggle, the moment of victory of our people!

Karabakh carpets

Karabakh carpets are an inspiration to design the logo of “Victory Watch”. The hourglass ornament described here is the main idea for the creation of the logo. This element shows that how our people attach high value on time.

Hour hands reﬂecting the colors of our ﬂag revolve around a board adorned with patterns of the Karabakh carpet and the word AZERBAIJAN.

So, the famous term KARABAKH IS AZERBAIJAN! is displayed in the design of the watch.

“Victory Watch” is put in a quality casket made of walnut. The handmade casket is tastefully designed. When it is opened, the image presented with the watch delivers a valuable concept.
